# 📄 Page Spec: Public Landing Page

**File Location**: `apps/web/app/page.tsx`  
**User Role**: Anonymous Visitor / Public

---

## 🎯 1. Purpose
Introduce the AgriVision platform to the public and farmers, highlighting the AI crop diagnosis and government initiative.

## 🧱 2. Required Sections
1.  **Navigation Bar**: Logo, Features, About, Alerts, Login (Pill-style button).
2.  **Hero Section**: 
    *   Main Headline: "Securing the Future of Bangladeshi Agriculture with AI."
    *   Sub-headline: "Instant crop disease diagnosis and expert advisory at your fingertips."
    *   CTA: Primary "Start Diagnosis" button (glow effect).
3.  **Core Feature Grid**:
    *   AI Diagnosis (Upload & Scan)
    *   Expert Network (Human verification)
    *   Regional Monitoring (Real-time maps)
4.  **How it Works**: Simple 3-step visualization (Upload -> Analyze -> Treat).
5.  **Outbreak Alert Banner**: A subtle, high-priority banner showing recent pest alerts in Bangladesh.
6.  **Footer**: Government links, social, and privacy.

## 💠 3. Key Components
- `GlassCard`: For feature highlights.
- `PulseBadge`: For live alert indicators.
- `HeroGradient`: The background mesh gradient.
